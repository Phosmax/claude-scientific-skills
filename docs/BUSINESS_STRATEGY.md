# AuraMax Business Strategy & Capabilities

## 1. Executive Summary
AuraMax is positioned as the **"Operating System for Precision Medicine"**, unifying the fragmented landscape of clinical care, pharmaceutical research, and regulatory compliance into a single, AI-native platform. By leveraging advanced skills in molecular analysis, medical imaging, and evidence grading, AuraMax empowers 12 distinct healthcare personas to work collaboratively.
